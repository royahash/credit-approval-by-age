Narrative D3 Visualization

Credit card approvals rise with applicant age in this anonymized dataset

This static lollipop chart compares approval rates across age groups. The story is not that age alone causes approval decisions; instead, the chart shows that in this dataset, older applicant groups had noticeably higher approval rates than younger groups.

Data source: Credit Card Applications dataset from Kaggle, based on the anonymized Australian Credit Approval/Statlog data. The uploaded CSV was cleaned and grouped into data/credit_approval_by_age_cleaned.csv for this visualization.

Design note: Because most variables are anonymized as A1–A14, this visualization uses only the readable age field and the approval class.
